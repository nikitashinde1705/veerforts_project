About Maharashtrian Forts

Maharashtrian forts are the pride of our history, built by Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj and other Maratha rulers to protect the dream of Hindavi Swarajya – self-rule for the people. Each fort, from Raigad to Pratapgad, tells stories of courage, strategy, and architectural brilliance. They are not just stone walls but living symbols of Maharashtra’s valor, culture, and heritage.These forts were designed with military genius — hidden paths, strong gates, and water reservoirs ensured survival even during long sieges. They also became centers of administration, trade, and culture, showcasing the vision of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj for a strong and independent Maharashtra.
